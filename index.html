Spripps Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Spripps ("we", "us") is a place-first social discovery app that helps you see where your world is happening. This policy explains what information Spripps collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep it short because our data practices are simple.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from that provider. You then choose a username and may add a display name, bio, and avatar.
  • Location. When you share a moment, Spripps uses your device location (with your permission, while the app is in use) to find venues near you and to verify you are at the venue. Your coordinates are used for this check at the time of posting and are not stored with your moment and not tracked continuously. The only location attached to a moment is the venue you selected.
  • Media. Photos and short videos you capture or select for a moment, including an automatically generated preview image for videos. Camera, microphone, and photo library access are used only when you actively create a moment.
  • Social activity. The moments you publish (venue, vibe, media, time), the accounts you follow, and follow requests.

2. How long we keep it

  • Moments are ephemeral. A moment is publicly visible for 24 hours. Media files that are no longer referenced are automatically deleted by a daily cleanup process.
  • Account data (profile, follows) is kept while your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed after a short grace period during which you can restore it by signing back in. Released usernames are permanently reserved and are not reassigned.

3. How your information is used

  • To operate the core service: showing moments, venues, and anonymous place activity (for example, how many people are at a venue and its dominant vibe — shown without identifying anyone).
  • To enforce our rules, such as verifying you are physically near a venue when posting.
  • To respect your privacy settings: if your account is private, your moments and media are visible only to followers you have accepted. Your activity still contributes to anonymous venue counts.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not show ads and do not share or sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not track your location in the background.
  • We do not use third-party analytics trackers that profile you across other apps or websites.

5. Where your data lives

Spripps runs on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the European Union (eu-central-1, Frankfurt). Data is encrypted in transit. Media files are stored in a private bucket and served only through short-lived signed links to people who are allowed to see them.

6. Third-party services

  • Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In — authentication only. Their handling of your sign-in is governed by their own privacy policies.
  • Supabase — database, storage, and serverless functions (our backend).
  • Apple Maps — map display inside the app.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You can make your account private at any time.
  • You can remove followers, and unfollow accounts.
  • You can delete your own moments.
  • You can delete your account, which removes your data as described in section 2.
  • You may contact us to access or delete your data at the address below. If you are in the EU/EEA or a similar jurisdiction, you may also have rights to correction, portability, and objection.

8. Children

Spripps is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the date above. Material changes will be communicated in the app.
